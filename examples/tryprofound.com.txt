# Profound

> AI search optimization platform for enterprise brands

## What is Profound?

Profound is an AI search optimization platform that helps enterprise brands improve their visibility across AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and others. The platform enables brands to reach millions of consumers who are using AI to discover new products and brands.

## Key Features

### Answer Engine Insights
Track how AI search engines mention your brand across different queries and conversations.

### Workflows
Create AI-optimized content quickly with automated tools designed for AI search visibility.

### Prompt Volumes
Analyze AI search trends to understand what users are asking AI engines about your industry.

### Agent Analytics
Monitor and understand how AI crawlers access your content to optimize for better AI visibility.

### Shopping Visibility
Optimize product placement and visibility in AI-powered shopping results.
